# Restaurant Hub

Version: 0.1

Status: Planning

Author: Evi Team

---

# 1. Project Overview

Restaurant Hub is a cloud-based Software as a Service (SaaS) platform that enables restaurants to digitize the dine-in ordering experience.

Customers scan a QR code placed on their table using their mobile phone, browse the restaurant's digital menu, place an order, and follow the order status in real time.

Restaurant staff receive the order immediately through the management dashboard, where they can prepare, manage, and complete orders without manually taking them at the table.

The platform is designed to support multiple restaurants from a single application.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Many restaurants still rely on waiters to manually take customer orders.

This creates several problems:

- Customers wait for a waiter before placing an order.
- Waiters spend significant time walking between tables and the kitchen.
- Incorrect orders can occur due to miscommunication.
- Restaurants have limited data about customer behavior.
- Updating printed menus is time-consuming and expensive.

Restaurant Hub solves these problems through QR-based ordering.
